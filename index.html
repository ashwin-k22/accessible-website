About me

Howdy! My name is Ashwin Kundeti. I am a Computer Engineering Major and I'm planning on pursuing a Masters in it. I am very fond of electronics and software in general. I am also a huge fan of the Texas A&M Aggies and I love to watch their football games. Gig 'em! In terms of interests in accessibility, I am interested in Screen Reader Technologies since I feel many blind users are missing out on a lot of content which people without disabilities can access. I would like to allow more blind users to have access to more content and resources and this is one technology very helpful for them.
